Egils Saga

(For the complete saga: The Icelandic Saga Database)

 

The saga tells the story of Egil Skallagrímsson who killed his first person in his early youth, he was a known Viking and poet.

The saga is told in the Settlement museum in Borgarnes.

Several pieces for the Egils Saga are of my hand and can be seen at the museum, Brákarbraut 13 – 15, Borgarnes
