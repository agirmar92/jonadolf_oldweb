Sameining

 

Untiy

 

2015

 

 

Marble

Granite

 

 

 

70 x 21 x 18 cm.

 

 

 

 

I hold your hand

and you hold mine

and so we go till

the end of time

 

we share our path

and along the way

we keep the faith

the struggles we slay

 

together we’re strong

no matter how long

no matter how far

united we are
